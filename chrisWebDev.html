About us

Via the Chris Mehn non-profit organization, we aim to encourage and equipe the next generation of leaders through the life lessons of Sports. Our organization aims to highlight those talents and fostor them to help that individual become an even better version of themselves. We offer several camps such as Football, Soccer, and Programming all with the goal of taking the youth on an adventure of exploring their talent and expanding upon that God given ability

Why this project

  • With the trenmendous growth in popularity of the Twitter platform, I was intregged with how the platform uses it's users' data. Instead of always going on the platform to do look at tweets .To better comprehend this, I set forth to obtain legal credentials from the platform and research how I can formally obtain wanted information via code. My language of choice for this project is Python.
    • Coding with Python, I am able to access my specific twitter account API using credentials I was able to apply for and obtain. After I got such credentials, I then imported required libraries through my Jupyter Notebook development environment. I also was intrigged with how specific tweets or content appear more on my timeline than others. In order to accurately depict my theory that contents appear on my timeline based on other contents that I view for a period of time and contents I live, I accessed specifics of tweets on my timeline. By doing such, I discovered:
    • I viewed similar contents
    • I bookmarked or saved contents are showcased similar characteristics
    • I retweeted/interacted with contents are similar
    Because of the listed reasons, the artifical intelligence aspect of the platform has flooded my timeline with similar contents.
